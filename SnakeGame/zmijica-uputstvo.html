The game of Snake is a simple game that has a snake and food that spawns on the table. You can select the speed of the snake and the size of the table by clicking on the buttons above.

The rules are simple: When you eat the regular food (color Red), the length of the snake extends by 1, and your score increases by 1. When you eat the super food, your score goes up by 10.

Clicking on the results button will take you to the high-score results page.
